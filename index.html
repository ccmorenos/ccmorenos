About Me

Hi! My name is Catalina Sarria, I love to understand how the world works, specially the quantum world. As child, I always dreamed of becoming a great scientist. Now, I'm achieving that dream working in the FENYX-UN investigation group in particle physics, researching b decays with data of the LHCb experiment.

Although my main interest is particle and high energy physics, I have worked in software development too. I've always been interested in programming, but I never thought that I'd end up working on it. Until now, I've been in projects as data analyst, applications developer and backend developer.

Now, I'm looking forward to initiate my master in particle physics, and keep improving my programming skills.
